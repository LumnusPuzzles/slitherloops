Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Lumnus Puzzles ("we", "our") provides the Slitherloops game as a Free app. This page informs you of our policies regarding data collection.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect personal data directly. However, the app uses third-party SDKs that may collect information used to identify you:

  • AdMob: Collects Advertising IDs and approximate location for personalized ads.
  • Firebase: Collects diagnostics and device identifiers to provide the "Daily Puzzle" functionality.

2. Data Deletion Request

We do not use user accounts; therefore, we do not store personal profiles. To request the deletion of third-party diagnostic data or advertising identifiers associated with your device, please contact us.

Contact Email: lumnuspuzzles@gmail.com

3. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
